About Me

Hello! I'm Kennedy Nomo, but you can call me Ken Is Nerdy. I am a passionate tech enthusiast based in Accra, Ghana. Over the past one and a half years, I have been exploring and excelling in various fields within the tech industry.
As a Tech Content Creator, I draw inspiration from top creators like MKBHD and Mr. whosetheboss. Sharing my knowledge and expertise, I've amassed around 5000 followers across social media and YouTube.
In the realm of Video Editing, I proudly serve as the Deputy Video Editor of Africaniwa, where we promote Africa's rich culture to the diaspora.
As Chief Front-end Developer at Akomapa Care, I lend my top-notch web design skills to provide care and accompanying services to individuals and households. My journey has been entirely self-taught, driven by an insatiable thirst for tech knowledge and creativity.
Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Let's embark on this tech adventure together!
